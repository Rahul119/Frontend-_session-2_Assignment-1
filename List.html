Scrambled Eggs


Eggs are one of my favorite food.Here is recipe for deliciously rich scrambled Eggs.


Ingredient


  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Mothod


  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the egg and cream in the bow.
  3. Once butter has melted mix the cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spetuala fold the egg from the edge of the pan to the centre.
